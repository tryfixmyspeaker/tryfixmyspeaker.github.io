About TryFixMySpeaker

Revolutionizing speaker maintenance with innovative sound wave technology, making professional-grade audio care accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free.

Our Story

TryFixMySpeaker was born from a simple frustration: watching perfectly good devices suffer from muffled speakers after minor water exposure or dust accumulation. Traditional solutions were either time-consuming (rice method), expensive (repair shops), or ineffective (air blowing).

We discovered that sound wave technology, specifically calibrated frequencies, could effectively dislodge water droplets and dust particles from speaker membranes. What started as a technical experiment evolved into a mission to democratize speaker care and make this powerful technology freely available to everyone.

Today, TryFixMySpeaker has helped hundreds of thousands of users restore their device audio quality, saving them time, money, and preventing unnecessary electronic waste.

The Science Behind Our Tool

TryFixMySpeaker leverages cutting-edge web technologies and acoustic science to deliver professional-grade speaker cleaning:

üîä Web Audio API

We use the powerful Web Audio API to generate precise frequencies with sub-hertz accuracy. This allows us to create the exact sound waves needed for effective water ejection and dust removal without requiring any app installation.

üåä Water Eject Technology

Our 165Hz frequency is specifically calibrated to create optimal speaker membrane oscillation. This vibration generates enough force to push water droplets out through speaker grilles while remaining completely safe for speaker components.

üí® Multi-Frequency Dust Removal

Dust removal requires a different approach. We cycle through multiple frequencies (200Hz to 6400Hz) to ensure comprehensive cleaning across the entire audio spectrum, dislodging particles of various sizes.

üì≥ Vibration Enhancement

For stubborn blockages, our tool combines audio frequencies with the Vibration API to provide dual-action cleaning. The mechanical vibration complements acoustic cleaning for maximum effectiveness.

Using stereo panning technology, we can target individual speakers (left or right) or both simultaneously. This precision ensures efficient cleaning and avoids unnecessary processing of unaffected speakers.
