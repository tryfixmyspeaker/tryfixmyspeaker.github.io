Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

Welcome to TryFixMySpeaker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information when you use our speaker cleaner tool. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

TryFixMySpeaker operates as a client-side web application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All processing happens locally in your web browser.

1.2 Device Information

Our speaker cleaner tool may access the following device features solely for functionality purposes:

  • Audio API: To generate sound frequencies for water eject and dust removal
  • Vibration API: To activate vibration mode for enhanced cleaning (only if you select this mode)
  • Browser Information: Basic browser type and version for compatibility purposes

This information is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics through standard web analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our website. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Referral sources

This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Service Functionality: To enable the speaker cleaner, water eject, and dust removal features
  • Website Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve our tool's effectiveness
  • Technical Support: To identify and resolve technical issues
  • User Experience: To optimize the interface and user experience across different devices

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Processing

All speaker cleaning functions (water eject, dust removal, vibration mode) are performed entirely within your browser using Web Audio API and other standard web technologies. No audio data or device information is uploaded to our servers.

3.2 Cookies and Local Storage

We may use cookies and local storage for the following purposes:

  • Remembering your preferences (such as preferred cleaning mode)
  • Analytics and website performance monitoring
  • Improving user experience on repeat visits

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect the core functionality of our speaker cleaner tool.

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect our website, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for secure connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

4. Third-Party Services

TryFixMySpeaker may utilize the following third-party services:

4.1 Analytics Services

We may use Google Analytics or similar services to collect anonymous usage statistics. These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

4.2 Content Delivery Networks (CDN)

We may use CDNs to deliver fonts, stylesheets, and other static resources for improved performance. These services may log basic access information.

4.3 Hosting Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses, access times, and pages visited. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your privacy:

  • Access: Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal information to access
  • Control: You can control browser permissions for audio and vibration features
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics tracking using browser extensions or privacy settings
  • Deletion: You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings

6. Children's Privacy

TryFixMySpeaker is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. Our speaker cleaner tool requires no registration or personal information submission, making it safe for all ages to use under appropriate supervision.

7. International Users

Our website is accessible globally. Since we don't collect or store personal information on our servers, there are no international data transfer concerns. All processing happens locally on your device, regardless of your location.

8. Contact Form Privacy

If you contact us through our contact form or email:

  • We will collect only the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message)
  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • We do not share your contact information with third parties
  • We retain contact information only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Material changes to this Privacy Policy will be prominently posted on our website. Your continued use of TryFixMySpeaker after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Data Retention

Since TryFixMySpeaker operates as a client-side application:

  • We do not retain any audio data or speaker information
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months
  • Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry
  • Cookies and local storage persist based on your browser settings

11. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. While we respect your privacy choices, our website may not respond to DNT signals as there is no industry standard for how to respond to them. However, since we collect minimal data, your privacy is protected regardless.

12. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since TryFixMySpeaker does not collect or sell personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply. We do not:

  • Sell personal information to third parties
  • Collect sensitive personal information
  • Use personal information for targeted advertising

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since our tool processes data locally on your device and we don't collect personal information, GDPR concerns are minimal. However, we uphold the following principles:

  • Lawfulness and Transparency: We are transparent about our minimal data practices
  • Data Minimization: We collect only what's necessary for analytics
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is used only for stated purposes
  • Storage Limitation: Anonymous analytics data is retained for limited periods

14. Your Consent

By using TryFixMySpeaker, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of our website and speaker cleaner tool.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Website: Contact Form
  • Project: GitHub - tryfixmyspeaker

We will respond to privacy inquiries within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days.

Summary: TryFixMySpeaker is committed to protecting your privacy. Our speaker cleaner tool operates entirely in your browser without collecting personal information. We use minimal anonymous analytics to improve our service. Your audio data never leaves your device, and you maintain complete control over your information.
